Our Story Began With a Simple Dream

In 2015, founders Maya and Tom Chen transformed a forgotten corner space into what is now Portland's most beloved neighborhood cafe. What started as a passion project has grown into a community hub.

We believe that great coffee should be accessible to everyone, served in a space that feels like an extension of your living room. That's why we've created multiple seating areas, from quiet reading nooks to communal tables perfect for catching up with friends.
