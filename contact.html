Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

We operate on a first-come, first-served basis for regular visits. For private events or large groups (8+), please contact us in advance.

Do you sell whole bean coffee?

Yes! We sell bags of our house blend and rotating single-origin selections. Ask your barista about current offerings.

Are you pet-friendly?

Well-behaved dogs are welcome on our outdoor patio. We even have water bowls and the occasional dog treat!

Do you have gluten-free options?

We offer several gluten-free pastries daily. Please let us know about any allergies when ordering.

Can I work from the cafe?

Absolutely! We have plenty of outlets, fast Wi-Fi, and a welcoming atmosphere for remote workers and students.

Do you have a loyalty program?

Yes! Pick up a punch card at the register. Every 10th drink is free.
